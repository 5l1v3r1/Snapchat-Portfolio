Summary


Although Snapchat attracts the youth the most, it's audience is constantly growing.

According to Statista, Snapchat has 229 million daily active users! That's only 100 million less than the population of the United States of America!

Following Snapchat's SDK (Software Development Kit), over 210 million snaps are created on Snapchat every day!

Another interesting statistic released by Statista is that 61% of Snapchat users are female, and 39% are male.


Key Features

  • Camera Communication

    • Snapchat takes advantage of the camera to add a new dimension
    to communication, letting people share the world through their own
    eyes. This inspires creativity in communication that can even be
    enhanced through the multiple stickers, emojis, drawings and gifs
    that are offered.

  • Discover Page

    • The discover page allows users to get lost in a new world
    filled with customized information. In the discover page, you
    can view stories from your friends, subscribe to news channels
    and find new content to follow.

  • Streaks

    • Creating an almost addicting incentive to stay active on the
    social media application was one of the most effective forms to
    retain daily users. The more people chat on the app, the more
    feedback the app gives them!

  • Score

    • The more people you talk to, the higher your score goes. Or,
    more specifically, your score is the combined number of "Snaps"
    you have sent and received (not counting stories).
